EMBRACE NEW CULTURES & EXPERIENCES IN YOUR TRAVELS

Many will know us as frequent travellers who enjoy exploring the cities around the world. With so many commercialised accommodation platforms, one could only explore this much, and this is where we want to make a bigger difference.

We started hosting our studio apartments on AirBnB in Johor Bahru, Malaysia. Since then, we have hosted multiple apartments, mainly at the KSL City (the heart of Johor Bahru), and have started hiring a team of professionals to assist us in running the daily operations.

At Royale Homestay, we understand that every traveller is looking to stay in a safe and clean environment while exploring the city, experiencing its local culture from dusk to dawn. What truly makes us stand out from the rest is that we are not only a list of apartments but we are also bringing home-level comfort and local culture to you.

With this motto in mind, Royale Homestay has achieved incredible hosting standards, with more than two hundred 5-star reviews and has been credited AirBnB Superhost status (which is a reflection of Royale Homestay's unique style of hospitality and shows the host providing a shining example for other hosts and extraordinary experiences for their guests) since December 2016.

Our team greatly looks forward to sharing our expertise in hosting on AirBnB, looking at regions such as Johor Bahru, Singapore and beyond.

Your adventure has only just begun.

- Royale Homestay
